Facilities

All of our Facilities are Glown here

Stage Hall

The marriage hall stage is an important part of any wedding ceremony. The stage is typically decorated with flowers, drapes, and lights to create a romantic atmosphere. It is a decorated platform that serves as the centerpiece of the wedding reception. It is where the couple sits during the entire event and is often the focal point of the stage decoration.

Stage Details : Stage Hall consists of 36x45 square feet where we can have 50 members without Spacial Difficulties in the Stage.


Seating Hall

The seating hall in the marriage hall, Subasree Mahal, offers a beautiful and spacious area for guests to gather and celebrate. With its elegant decor and comfortable seating arrangements, the seating hall creates a warm and inviting atmosphere for everyone attending the wedding.

Seating Details : Seating Capacity of the Hall is 1000 Person without Spacial Issues and the Size of the Seating Hall is 100x80 Square Feet.


Dining Hall

The dining hall in the marriage hall is a spacious and elegant space that can accommodate a large number of guests. The room is beautifully decorated with chandeliers, giving it a luxurious ambiance. The tables are elegantly set with fine linens and exquisite tableware.

Dining Details : This Dining Hall contains 200+ Chairs and 39x130 Square Feet Large Respectively.


Buffet Hall

Subasree Mahal is a popular marriage hall that also features a buffet hall. With its elegant decor and excellent catering services, Subasree Mahal ensures that all guests have a wonderful dining experience.

Buffet Area Details : We can serve 350 Persons at Live and the size of the Buffet Area is 140x80 Square Feet Long.


Parking Area

Subasree Mahal offers a dedicated parking area for guests attending events at their marriage hall. The parking area is conveniently located nearby and provides ample space for vehicles. This ensures that guests have a hassle-free experience and can easily park their cars while attending functions at Subasree Mahal.

Parking Details : Parking Area space has capacity to Park 300+ Cars without Spacial Disturbance.
